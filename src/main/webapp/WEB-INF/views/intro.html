Simple Web Integer Calculator

Instruction:

  1. enter params for "/calc?" URL:

    1. "operation=" and enter one from
      • "a" for "+" (addition)
      • "s" for "-" (subtraction)
      • "m" for "*" (multiplication)
      • "d" for "/" (division)
    2. "operandOne=" and enter the first integer number
    3. "operandTwo=" and enter the second integer number

  2. or calc predefined expression:

    • "9+3" that is equal to "../calc?operation=a&operandOne=9&operandTwo=3"
    • "9-3" that is equal to "../calc?operation=s&operandOne=9&operandTwo=3"
    • "9*3" that is equal to "../calc?operation=m&operandOne=9&operandTwo=3"
    • "9/3" that is equal to "../calc?operation=d&operandOne=9&operandTwo=3"
